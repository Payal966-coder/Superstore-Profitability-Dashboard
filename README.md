## 📌 Business Case Overview
In the competitive retail landscape, understanding the relationship between sales volume and net profitability is crucial. This project presents a comprehensive data analysis of a global superstore's supply chain operations. 

The primary goal was to identify "Profit Leaks" and evaluate the impact of discounting strategies across different global markets. By transforming over 50,000 rows of transactional data into this interactive dashboard, I’ve provided a clear roadmap for cost optimization and margin improvement.

## 📈 Strategic Business Insights
The analysis yielded high-impact findings for stakeholders:

* **Financial Performance:** Successfully tracked a total revenue of **$12.6M** with a net profit of **$1.4M**. The current **11.6% Profit Margin** serves as the baseline for future optimizations.
* **Loss Mitigation:** Identified critical negative margin products. Specifically, **Cubify 3D Printers** and certain **Furniture lines** (Bevis Computer Tables) were found to be depleting overall profits, indicating a need for urgent supply chain or pricing restructuring.
* **Discount Threshold Analysis:** Data modeling revealed that **discounts exceeding 20%** lead to a sharp decline in profitability, often resulting in net losses per transaction.
* **Regional Deep-Dive:** While the **Central Region** drives the highest sales volume ($2.8M), regions like **Southeast Asia** show high operational costs that suppress net margins despite significant sales activity.
* **Customer Segment Value:** The **Consumer segment** accounts for over **50%** of total profit, highlighting the most lucrative market for targeted marketing campaigns.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
* **ETL Processes (Power Query):** Cleaned and transformed multi-source data, ensuring zero null values and standardized categorical fields for accurate reporting.
* **Data Modeling:** Developed a star-schema inspired model in Excel to allow seamless cross-filtering between geography, time, and product categories.
* **Advanced Visualizations:** Leveraged Waterfall charts for loss tracking, Donut charts for segment contribution, and trend lines for discount impact analysis.
* **UX/UI Optimization:** Designed a professional Teal-Grey interface focused on data hierarchy and readability for executive-level decision-making.

## 🚀 How to Interact with the Dashboard
1. **Download** the repository.
2. Open `superstore_cleaned_complete.xlsx` in **Microsoft Excel**.
3. Use the **Slicers on the right** (Region, Country, Category) to filter the entire dashboard dynamically.
4. Review the **Loss Analysis** tab to identify specific items impacting the bottom line.
